Profile

Measured advice for complex tax and regulatory decisions.

Practicing lawyer in taxation matters, registered with the Bar Council of Punjab & Haryana, Chandigarh.

Jai Bhagwan Saini is a seasoned Tax Consultant and Advocate with 19 years of hands-on experience (In guidance of late Sh Madan Lal, Late Sh Satya Dev Sharma & Ms Yogita) advising individuals, HUFs, and corporate clients on complex taxation and regulatory matters across India.

He specialises in end-to-end income tax return filing and scrutiny assessments for high-net-worth individuals and businesses, and advises on tax-efficient structures and investment planning to minimise tax liabilities.

He regularly represents clients before appellate authorities — the Commissioner of Income Tax (CIT), NCLT, CBDT, Income Tax Appellate Tribunal (ITAT), GST and VAT authorities, and the High Court. He also assists in the formation of HUF, Partnership Firms, LLP, OPC, Private Limited and Limited Companies as per client needs.
